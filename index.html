Born & Raised.

Before the "Panzoti" became a local legend, it was just a secret family recipe shared in a small kitchen in Beaver Dam. Drafthouse Pizza wasn't built by a corporation; it was built by the neighborhood.

For decades, we've stayed true to our roots: high-quality ingredients, dough made fresh every single morning, and a commitment to being the place where the community gathers for the big moments and the quiet ones.

Small town heart. Big time flavor.
