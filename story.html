Our Story

From a single brick oven in Beaver Dam to a community institution, we've been serving the soul of Wisconsin on a pizza tray for four decades.

40 Years of Craft

Beaver Dam Born & Raised

In 1984, the Drafthouse wasn't a brand; it was a promise. A promise to provide the working people of Beaver Dam a place to kick back, share a cold one, and experience the best dough in the Midwest.

Founded by the Miller family, our roots are deep in this soil. We've seen the town change, but our commitment to the local grain, the local families, and the local vibe hasn't budged an inch.
